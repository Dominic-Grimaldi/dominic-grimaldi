Correspondences and Decision-Making Part 1

This project was part of a larger assignment in my Publishing and Editing Capstone meant to simulate a real-world interaction between an editor and multiple aspects of the publishing and editing industry. In this section, we were asked to simulate accepting and rejecting pieces for an imaginary magazine. From there, we wrote acceptance and rejection letters based on our pre-selected criteria with the goal of delivering gracious but informative feedback.
